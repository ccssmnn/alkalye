# Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

## Data Storage

Your documents are stored:

- Locally on your device
- Optionally synced to the [Jazz Cloud](https://jazz.tools) when you create an account

## Encryption

Your data is end-to-end encrypted:

- Encryption keys are derived from your passphrase
- Your passphrase is the only key to decrypt your data
- Neither we nor Jazz can access your documents
- When you share documents, collaborators can decrypt them using their own passphrases

## Analytics

We do not track you or collect analytics data.
